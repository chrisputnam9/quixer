Quixer - About this App

Overview

Quixer is a Quick Search tool. It combines features inspired by Custom Search Engines, DuckDuckGo's "Bangs", and other tools. Quixer adds syncing of customizations across browsers & devices.

The current release provides:

  • An interface to manage and sync up your services (search URLs)
  • A selection of default services for popular sites
  • Ability to integrate with your browser's search bar
  • Ability to sync your custom services/URLs across browsers/devices via Google

Get Started

Go to https://quixer.onl/#config, customize the services you want to use, and set up syncing with Google Drive (optional).

Set up https://quixer.onl?q=%s as your default custom search engine (eg. in Chrome - chrome://settings/searchEngines)

Start searching, for example, try "map nearest restaurant" in your search bar

Privacy

This app does not collect your data. It saves your configuration to your own Google Drive if you sign in. It doesn't track analytics, usage, history, or anything else.
