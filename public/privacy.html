Quixer - Privacy Policy

Quixer App

Quixer (quixer.onl) does not share your data with anyone.

  • Quixer stores your application configuration in your browser for your own use.
  • Quixer uses your Google login to sync your application configuration to your own Google Drive account if you sign in on the Quixer config page.
    To facilitate this, Quixer stores tokens and IDs in your browser that maintain the connection between your own browser and your Google account.
  • Quixer does not use, store or share any of your Google user data.
  • Quixer does not share your configuration, usage, history, or any other data with the application developers or with anyone else.
    We frankly don't want it.
