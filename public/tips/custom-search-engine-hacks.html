Custom Search Engine Hacks

Quixer App | About Quixer | All Tips

Another way to think of a custom search engine: it is a URL with a "slot" in it that is filled in with the text you enter in your URL bar.

Typically the text you enter would be a search phrase, and the URL would go to a search results page - but that doesn't have to be the case.

Here are some ideas to get you started!

Example Keyword URL
Simple Keyboard Shortcuts
Calendar cal https://calendar.google.com/calendar/r/month
GMail Drafts drafts https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#search/in:draft
